What we do

We stand for equality and justice, working on issues such as women's empowerment, defending the rights of the marginalised, improving livelihoods, accessing natural resources and holding government to account.
CORO Overview
Grassroot leadership
Developing grassroots leaders to tackle challenging issues in their communities and beyond.
Gender Sensitization
Gender sensitization in schools to address attitudes and behaviours before they are fully formed.​
Empowering women
Empowering women from marginalised, low income communities to act against violence and work together in solidarity.
Regional Campaigns
Grassroots-led region-specific initiatives such as single women's issues, gender-equal sanitation, and scarcity
